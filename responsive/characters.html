Meet the Mystery Inc. Gang

Scooby-Doo

Voiced: Don Messick (1969–1996)

A lovable, goofy Great Dane with a big appetite and an even bigger heart. Though easily scared, Scooby always comes through for his friends—especially when bribed with a Scooby Snack! His signature catchphrase? “Ruh-roh, Raggy!”

Shaggy Rogers

Voiced: Casey Kasem (1969–1997, 2002–2009)

Scooby’s best friend and the most food-obsessed member of Mystery Inc. Shaggy is laid-back, cowardly, and always looking for his next meal. Despite his fear of ghosts and monsters, he somehow ends up in the middle of every spooky mystery.

Fred Jones

Voiced: Frank Welker (1969–present)

The brave and determined leader of the gang. Fred is known for setting traps (which sometimes work!) and keeping the team focused on solving mysteries. He drives the Mystery Machine and often takes charge when things get tough.

Daphne Blake

Voiced: Stefanianna Christopherson (1969), Heather North (1970–1997)

Stylish and resourceful, Daphne is more than just a fashionista—she’s fearless and always willing to put herself in danger to uncover the truth. While she sometimes gets into trouble, she’s great at thinking on her feet and finding solutions.

Velma Dinkley

Voiced: Nicole Jaffe (1969–1973)

The brains of the operation, Velma is a genius when it comes to solving puzzles and decoding clues. With her signature orange sweater and thick glasses, she’s always one step ahead of the mystery—though she frequently loses her glasses at the worst moments!
